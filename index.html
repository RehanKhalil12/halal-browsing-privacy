Privacy Policy – Halal Browsing

Effective Date: 01-01-2026

1. Data Collection

Halal Browsing does not collect any personal or identifiable information. The extension does not track browsing history, location, communications, or any user activity.

2. Data Processing

All face detection and blurring are performed locally in the user’s browser. No images, text, or web content are transmitted to any external server.

3. Permissions

The extension requests access to webpage content solely to detect adult faces and apply the blur effect. These permissions are used exclusively for this purpose.

4. Third-Party Services
